## Overview

The SRCB Junior High School Department Promotional Website was designed and developed to enhance the school's online presence, providing prospective students and parents with essential information about programs, events, and achievements. Built on WordPress, the website features a user-friendly interface, dynamic content management, and responsive design to ensure accessibility across all devices.

## Key Features

- **Custom WordPress Design**: Tailored layout and theme to reflect the school's identity.
- **Responsive & Mobile-Friendly**: Ensures smooth browsing on desktops, tablets, and smartphones.
- **Content Management System (CMS)**: Allows school staff to easily update news, announcements, and events.
- **Multimedia Integration**: Supports images, videos, and downloadable resources for an engaging experience.
- **SEO Optimization**: Improves search engine visibility for better reach.

## Technologies Used

- **Platform**: WordPress
- **Theme Customization**: PHP, HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- **Database**: MySQL
- **Plugins Used**: Elementor, Yoast SEO, Contact Form
- **Hosting & Deployment**: Apache/Ngingx

## Challenges and Learnings

The development process required customizing WordPress beyond basic templates to align with the school's branding and functional requirements. Ensuring responsiveness and accessibility for different devices was a priority. Optimizing performance and SEO was another key challenge to improve visibility and user engagement. This project strengthened my skills in WordPress customization, UI/UX design, and website optimization techniques.

## Outcome

The website successfully enhanced SRCB Junior High School’s online presence, making essential school information easily accessible to students and parents. The user-friendly CMS empowered school staff to manage content efficiently, while SEO optimization improved search rankings, increasing visibility. Overall, the project provided a modern, professional, and engaging platform to promote the school’s programs and activities.
